How Kennard Works

These FAQs are written specifically for how our small town operates — from wastewater payments and trash service to Sheriff and Volunteer Fire Department procedures.

How do I pay my wastewater bill?

Wastewater payments can be made online using pay.gov. The bill includes one tote per account.

Pay Wastewater Online

How do I get an additional trash tote?

Additional totes can be rented from Bestway for an additional fee. Contact Best Way of Indiana directly at (765) 853-5714 or visit www.bestway-disposal.com.

Who do I call for animal issues?

Henry County Animal Control handles all animal-related concerns during business hours: (765) 529-8131.
After hours, contact the Henry County Sheriff non-emergency line: (765) 521-7032.

What are the speed limits in Kennard?

All town streets are 25 mph. State Road 234 is 35 mph within town limits. Please drive safely.

When does the Town Council meet?

The Town Board meets on the second Wednesday of every month at 7:00 PM at Town Hall. Agendas are posted on the Minutes & Agendas page.

How do I contact the Clerk/Treasurer?

Clerk/Treasurer Jan Lockridge
Phone: (765) 785-2331
Text: (765) 524-0646
Email: clerk@kennardin.com

Please contact during regular business hours.

How do I submit a sealed bid?

Bids must be sealed and clearly marked on the envelope. They can be hand-delivered to the Clerk or placed in the sewer payment drop box at Town Hall. Full instructions are on the Bids & Info page.

How do I contact the Volunteer Fire Department?

For general (non-emergency) questions: vfd@kennardin.com
For all emergencies: Dial 911
