Town Utilities Overview

The Town of Kennard provides wastewater service and coordinates trash collection for residents.

Electric service is provided by Duke Energy. Natural gas service is provided by Fountain Town Gas Co. A second trash tote can be rented directly from Best Way of Indiana.
