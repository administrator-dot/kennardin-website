  • Accident Reports : $10

  • Lifetime Personal Protection Gun Permit (Local Fee) : $50

Payment Information

  • Payment Methods:
  • By Appointment : Pay via check to the Clerk.
  • Online : Pay by credit card anytime through our secure payment portal (link below).

Important Payment Guidelines:

  • Payments must be completed prior to your service appointment.
  • Bring your receipt or payment confirmation to your appointment.
  • Officers cannot accept payments in cash or check. Only the Clerk can process checks.

Online Payment Instructions:

  1. Use the button below to access the payment portal.
  2. If paying for a service fee, ignore references to "violations" or "ordinances."
  3. For "Violation Date," you can select today's date to proceed.

Please ensure all payments are finalized before your appointment to avoid delays. If you forget to pay in advance, use the online portal to complete your payment.

Received an Ordinance Violation?

Ordinance Violations, Clerk Fees, Permit Fees and other Town related payments can be made by credit card at this link below:

Ordinance violations, not traffic violations, can be paid by check or money order at the Clerk's Office in the Town Hall. We will also accept Credit Card payments at PayGov.us (a small fee will be added to process the credit card which does not go to the Town)

Vehicle infraction tickets are handled through the state. Check the ticket for payment information.

The Clerks have ZERO knowledge of police matters. Traffic violations paid to the above link will be returned. The Town of Kennard does not collect payment for vehicle infractions. Read the citation for more details.
