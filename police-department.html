Police Department

Kennard’s Law Enforcement

Our police department provides 24/7 protection for the Town of Kennard and surrounding areas.

How to Report a Crime or Suspicious Activity

Emergency (life or property in immediate danger): Call 911

Non-Emergency: Call 765-571-2640 or Henry County Sheriff at 765-521-7032

Safety Tips for Residents

  • Always keep your car doors locked when parked, even in your driveway.
  • Lock all windows and doors at night and when away from home.
  • Install outdoor lighting and trim bushes near entrances.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and report any suspicious activity immediately.

Traffic Safety & Parking

All town streets have a speed limit of 25 MPH.

State Road 234 has a speed limit of 35 MPH.

Please obey all posted signs and be especially cautious near schools and playgrounds.
