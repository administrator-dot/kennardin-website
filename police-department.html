Police Department

Kennard’s Law Enforcement

Our police department provides 24/7 protection and works closely with the Henry County Sheriff’s Office to keep our community safe.

Traffic Safety & Parking

All town streets have a speed limit of 25 MPH.

State Road 234 has a speed limit of 35 MPH within town limits.

Please obey all posted signs and be especially cautious near parks.

Safety Tips

  • Keep cars locked when parked.
  • Report suspicious activity immediately.
  • Never leave valuables visible in your vehicle.
