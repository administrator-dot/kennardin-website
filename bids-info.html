Current Bid Opportunity

Post Office & Town Hall Maintenance

Sealed bids due at least 48 hours before the next Town Board meeting.

Town of Kennard – Request for Sealed Bids

The Town of Kennard is accepting sealed bids for Post Office and Town Hall maintenance work.

Scope of Work

  • Replace two interior light fixtures in the Kennard Post Office with LED fixtures using the same footprint so that repainting is not required.
  • Repair the Post Office door closer.
  • Repair two Town Hall door closers.

The previous dusk-to-dawn light has been removed from the scope and is not part of this bid.

Bid Requirements

  • Bids must be sealed
  • The envelope must clearly state “USPS Repair Bid” on the outside
  • Bids must be received at least 48 hours before the next Town Board meeting
  • Bids will be opened publicly during the Town Board meeting

Submission & Viewing

Viewing the Work Area
Anyone interested in bidding may visit the Kennard Post Office between 12:30 PM and 4:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Submit Bids To
Town of Kennard
100 N. Main Street
Kennard, Indiana 47351
Hand deliver to the Clerk or place in the black deposit box on the south side of Town Hall.

The Town of Kennard reserves the right to reject any or all bids for any reason.
