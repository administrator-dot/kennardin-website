Kennard Volunteer Fire Department

Providing fire protection, rescue, and emergency medical services to Kennard and surrounding areas 24 hours a day.

About Fire Chief Ronald (Ron) Huffman

Ronald (Ron) Huffman began his fire service career in 1985 as a volunteer firefighter with the Shirley Volunteer Fire Department. He has served with the Kennard Volunteer Fire Department since 1995 and was appointed Fire Chief in 2019.

He has attended numerous fire training schools and holds certifications in many areas of fire service. Chief Huffman and his wife Debbie are longtime residents of the area.

General Fire Safety Tips

  • Install and maintain smoke detectors on every level of your home
  • Keep a working fire extinguisher accessible
  • Develop and practice a home fire escape plan
  • Never leave burning candles or outdoor fires unattended
