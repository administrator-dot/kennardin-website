Chief Ronald (Ron) Huffman

Ronald (Ron) Huffman started his fire service career in 1985 as a volunteer firefighter with the Shirley Vol. Fire Department and has been with the Kennard Volunteer Fire Department since 1995. He was appointed Fire Chief in 2019. Ron has attended numerous fire training schools and is certified in many areas of fire service. Ron is retired from the Henry County Emergency Management.

Fire Safety Tips

  • Install and maintain smoke detectors
  • Create and practice a home escape plan
  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources
  • Know how to use a fire extinguisher
