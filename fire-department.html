Chief Bio

Ronald (Ron) Huffman Fire Chief

Ronald (Ron) Huffman began his fire service career in 1985 as a volunteer firefighter with the Shirley Volunteer Fire Department. He has been with the Kennard Volunteer Fire Department since 1986 and was appointed Fire Chief in 2019. Ron served as a career firefighter with the New Castle Fire Department starting in 1989, advancing to Battalion Chief. He retired as Director of the Henry County Emergency Management Agency and served as Chairman of the Henry County Local Emergency Planning Committee. He is President of Responder Training, Inc. and provides specialized training throughout the region.
