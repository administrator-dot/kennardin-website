Chief Bio

Ron Huffman Fire Chief

Ronald (Ron) Huffman started his fire service career in 1985 as a volunteer firefighter with the Shirley Vol. Fire Department and has been with the Kennard Volunteer Fire Department since 1995. He was appointed Fire Chief in 2005. Ron has attended numerous fire training schools and is certified in many areas of fire service. Ron and his wife Debbie have been residents of Kennard since 1993.
