Chief Bio

Ron Huffman Fire Chief

Ronald (Ron) Huffman started his fire service career in 1985 as a volunteer firefighter with the Shirley Vol. Fire Department, when he moved he joined the Kennard Vol. Fire Department in 1986 and is still a member. From 1978 to 1986 he worked as a mechanic until the garage closed and was then hired at Paul Akers Inc., a propane and anhydrous ammonia engineering company.
