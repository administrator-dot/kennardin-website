Fire Department

Ronald (Ron) Huffman

Fire Chief

Chief Huffman has served with the Kennard Volunteer Fire Department since 1995 and has been Fire Chief since 2019.

The Kennard Volunteer Fire Department provides fire protection, rescue services, and emergency medical assistance to the Town of Kennard and surrounding areas.

The department is staffed entirely by dedicated volunteers who respond to emergencies 24 hours a day.

General Fire Safety Tips

  • Install and maintain smoke detectors on every level of your home
  • Keep a working fire extinguisher accessible
  • Develop and practice a home fire escape plan
  • Never leave burning candles or outdoor fires unattended

Contact the Fire Department
