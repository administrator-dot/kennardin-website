Local Schools

School Districts Near Kennard, IN 47351

The Town of Kennard is located within the Charles A. Beard Memorial School Corporation (approximately 9 miles south in Knightstown). Students can catch a bus directly in Kennard.

1. Charles A. Beard Memorial School Corporation (Primary District)

Distance: ~9 miles south (Knightstown)
Bus stop available directly in Kennard.
Official Corporation Website: www.cabeard.k12.in.us
Official Indiana State Report Card & Rankings: View on Indiana GPS

2. Shenandoah School Corporation

Distance: ~7 miles north
Closest bus stop is 1 mile north of Kennard at the Baptist Church parking lot.
Official Corporation Website: www.sscraiders.org
Official Indiana State Report Card & Rankings: View on Indiana GPS

3. New Castle Community School Corporation

Distance: ~10 miles east (New Castle)
Official Corporation Website: www.nccsc.k12.in.us
Official Indiana State Report Card & Rankings: View on Indiana GPS

4. Eastern Hancock Community School Corporation

Distance: ~15 miles southwest
Closest bus stop is in Shirley, Indiana (approximately 3.5 miles west of Kennard).
Official Corporation Website: www.easternhancock.org
Official Indiana State Report Card & Rankings: View on Indiana GPS

  • Girls Volleyball – State Champions 2007, 2017, 2018, 2019 (Class 4A)
