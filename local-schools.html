School District

The Town of Kennard is within the Charles A. Beard Memorial School Corporation (approximately 8 miles from Kennard to Knightstown campus).

Current Schools Serving Kennard

Kennard students attend Knightstown Elementary, Knightstown Intermediate, and Knightstown High School.

After the 1974 tornado, high school students were bussed to Knightstown. Kennard Elementary operated until its final year in 2013 and was sold in 2015 as part of centralization efforts.

School Bus Stop: Closest Knightstown bus stop for Kennard students is at the intersection of Main Street and State Road 234 (approximately 1.5 miles from central Kennard).

Memorable Local Alumni

Steve Alford

New Castle High School (Class of 1983)

Achievements: Indiana Mr. Basketball 1983 • NCAA National Champion 1987 (Indiana Hoosiers) • NBA player (Dallas Mavericks, Golden State Warriors) • Head coach at UCLA and New Mexico.

Local Importance: Led New Castle to back-to-back state championships in the mid-1980s. One of Indiana’s most iconic high school basketball legends and a proud New Castle graduate who put Henry County on the national map.

Kent Benson

New Castle High School

Achievements: NBA player (Milwaukee Bucks, Detroit Pistons) • NCAA champion with Indiana in 1976 • 3-time All-Big Ten selection.

Local Importance: Another New Castle basketball star who helped bring statewide recognition to the Trojans program in the 1970s.
