School District

The Town of Kennard is within the Charles A. Beard Memorial School Corporation.

Current Schools Serving Kennard

Kennard is served by Knightstown Elementary, Knightstown Intermediate, and Knightstown High School. The corporation is currently seeking to relocate the elementary and intermediate schools to the land where the current high school sits, creating a single campus for all buildings.

After the 1974 tornado, high school students were bussed to Knightstown. Kennard Elementary operated until its final year in 2013 and was sold in 2015 as part of centralization efforts.
