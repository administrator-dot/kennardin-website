Town of Kennard - Local Schools

Charles A. Beard Memorial School Corporation

The Town itself is part of the Charles A. Beard Memorial School Corporations district.

Your students can catch a bus directly in Kennard.

All of Charles A. Beard Memorial School Corporation schools are located in Knightstown, Indiana.

Knightstown is located about 9 miles South of Kennard.

Shenandoah School Corporation

The Shenandoah School Corporation is located about 7 miles North of Kennard.

The closest bus stop for students is located 1-mile North of Kennard at the Baptist Church Parking lot.

Eastern Hancock Community School Corporation

The Eastern Hancock Community School Corporation is located 15 miles Southwest of Kennard.

The closest bus stop for students is in our neighboring town of Shirley, Indiana.

Shirley is located about 3.5 miles West of Kennard.
