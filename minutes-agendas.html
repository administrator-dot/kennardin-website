Meeting Schedule

Town Council meetings are held on the second Wednesday of every month at 7:00 PM at Town Hall. All meetings are open to the public.

Understanding Agendas and Minutes

Agendas outline the topics planned for discussion at each meeting. Agendas are available before the meetings. They are often not digitally uploaded prior to the meeting. Attendees can have a copy at the meetings. Minutes provide the official record of what was discussed and decided during the meeting.
