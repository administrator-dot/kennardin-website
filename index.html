Welcome to Kennard, Indiana

Kennard is located in western Henry County and is a part of Greensboro Township. The town covers 0.42 square miles and has an estimated population of approximately 446 residents (2025). It was platted in 1882 and has three parks developed in the last ten years, along with a recent housing boom.
