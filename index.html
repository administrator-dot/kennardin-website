Kennard is located in western Henry County and is a part of Greensboro Township.

Kennard, Indiana (ZIP 47351) is a small, friendly community with a rich history dating back to its platting in 1882. The town has grown through the years and continues to see a housing boom. Over the past ten years, three new parks have been developed. During the COVID-19 pandemic, the town increased its medical volunteer personnel and purchased a newer vehicle to improve emergency medical assistance.
