Public Meeting Livestreaming and Recording Notice

The Town will livestream these meetings on its official YouTube channel at YouTube.

In the event of a technological failure that prevents livestreaming, the Town will attempt to record the audio of the meeting and make the recording available on the same YouTube channel for public access.

Links to the meeting’s agenda, minutes, or memoranda, if applicable will be on this site Minutes & Agendas.

Public Notice: Public Comments Policy

The Town of Kennard operates under an Open Door Policy, encouraging transparency and public participation. A public comment section is included on each month's agenda, where any member of the public may address the Board.

While anyone is welcome to speak during the public comment period, the Town Board encourages citizens to contact the Clerk's office at least 72 hours before a meeting to ensure their comments can be properly included on the agenda.

This policy complies with Indiana's Open Door Law (IC 5-14-1.5), ensuring that all meetings are open to public observation.
